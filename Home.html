Who Are We?

The Clutches Clan (commonly referred to as CL) is
a gaming clan owned by Paul S. Decoste. Clutches
means to overcome the difficult trials in gaming. CL
was created on January 1'st 2022 and will become a
gaming org some time in 2037. Learn more on the
"About Us" Page or the Discord Server.
